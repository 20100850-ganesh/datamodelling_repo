About My Dissertation: Promoting Socio-Economic Development Through Technology Adoption


Welcome to my dissertation project, where I embark on a mission to drive positive change in developing countries through the adoption of technology. As a student pursuing an M.Sc. in Computing (Enterprise Software Systems) at South East Technological University, Waterford, my focus lies in harnessing the power of technology to foster socio-economic development and improve the livelihoods of communities in need.


The motivation behind this project stems from my deep-rooted belief that technology can be a catalyst for progress. I have witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by farmers and communities in developing countries, particularly in the agricultural sector. By creating a proof of concept, I aim to demonstrate how cutting-edge computing methodologies can significantly impact the socio-economic landscape in these regions.


With a focus on agriculture, the backbone of many developing economies, my work centers on developing a novel system for detecting plant diseases in rice crops. By providing an accessible platform for farmers to detect diseases early on, we empower them to take timely action, minimize crop losses, and optimize their agricultural practices.

The impact of this dissertation goes beyond academia. It could hold the potential to make a real difference in the lives of farmers, helping them achieve sustainable growth and prosperity. By leveraging technology, we open doors to improved food security, increased crop yields, and enhanced economic opportunities for these communities.


I extend my heartfelt gratitude to my supervisor, faculty, and the university for their unwavering support throughout this journey. Additionally, I thank the communities who have generously contributed their knowledge and insights, making this research truly meaningful.


As I present this proof of concept, I invite you to be part of this impactful endeavor. Together, let us advocate for technology adoption and pave the way for a brighter and more inclusive future for all. Thank you for being part of this significant milestone in my academic journey. With your encouragement and collaboration, we can shape a world where technology bridges the gap and brings prosperity to those who need it most.


Note: This dissertation project is purely academic in nature, and my aim is to contribute knowledge and ideas towards promoting positive change.
