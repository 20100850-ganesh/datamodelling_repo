Rice Plant Disease Detection

As an aspiring innovator pursuing an M.Sc. in Computing, my dissertation work focuses on a transformative project aimed at benefiting farmers in developing countries, including my own. Through cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, I am dedicated to developing a state-of-the-art solution for Rice Plant Disease Detection.


The significance of rice in the socio-economic development of developing countries cannot be overstated. However, the devastating impact of plant diseases poses a constant threat to rice crops, leading to significant yield losses for farmers. My mission is to empower these farmers with accessible and affordable technology that can revolutionize disease diagnosis and management.


By simply uploading an image of a rice plant, my system will provide real-time and accurate disease identification. Timely detection enables farmers to implement targeted interventions, minimizing losses, and optimizing crop yield. My goal is to contribute to the betterment of the agricultural sector by harnessing the potential of technology for social good. By making advanced disease detection accessible to small-scale farmers, we can pave the way for sustainable agricultural practices, economic growth, and food security.
